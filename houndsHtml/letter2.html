They call him Death and only two people have beaten him in the past, Bryan and the man who took care of Bryan when your mother passed. I figured if the lesser Son of Asphalt can do it, what of the man who outran the streets?

Be careful out there, his challenge is gonna break you, you'll come out a different man. Whatever happens, don't give up.

With love and hope,
